Gravidy Comments Section

Developed backend functionality for the comments section including delete, edit, pin options, and more with tools such as Flask and Amazon DynamoDB.

Challenge What features will enhaunce the experience of a user in the comments section and how do they support Gravidy's mission?
My Role Feature Development, Design Documentation, Unit Testing
Year 2024 to Present
Timeline Over 5 months
Tools Flask, Amazon DynamoDB, Python, Pytest

Project Details

Objective

Create a comments section that is competitive with the industry standard and supports Gravidy's mission.

About Gravidy

Gravidy is a social media startup with a mission of fighting doom-scrolling and online burnout with features that allow users to take back agency over their algorithm.

My Contributions

As a Backend Engineer, I have played a key role in designing and implementing the comments system for Gravidy, ensuring it is scalable, efficient, and aligned with user needs. I contributed to key design documents for upcoming features and developed core functionalities, including:

✔
Comment Management – Users can edit, delete, and like comments seamlessly.
✔
Granular Comment Permissions – Customizable settings for who can comment, fostering safer discussions.
✔
Sentiment-Based Ranking – Prioritizes constructive and engaging comments through sentiment analysis.
✔
Spam & Toxicity Detection – AI-powered filtering to reduce harmful and irrelevant content.
✔
Pinned & Categorized Comments – Helps surface meaningful discussions and enhance engagement.

Impact & Technical Highlights

●
Built and optimized the DynamoDB schema for scalable comment storage.
●
Developed Flask-based APIs to support fast and reliable comment interactions.
●
Wrote unit and integration tests with Pytest, achieving high test coverage.
●
Integrated NLP models for sentiment analysis and content moderation.

This work has contributed to making Gravidy's comments section a safer, more engaging, and user-controlled experience, reinforcing its mission to transform social media for the better.
